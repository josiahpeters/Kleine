Rewards

Scoring

Each person who takes part in making a prediction will earn a score based on how many categories the guessed correctly. Categories are worth 2 to 5 points for a total of 15 points. The top two people with the most points will be rewarded with a prize!

Prediction Points
Gender 2 pts
Date 5 pts
Time 3 pts
Weight 3 pts
Length 2 pts
Total Points 15 pts

The prediction categories are assigned points in a way that benefit those who predict the correct date of delivery but misses the gender over someone who correctly predicts the gender but misses the deliver date.

First Prize

The person with the most points gets a $50 visa gift card!

Second Prize

The person with the second most points gets a $25 visa gift card!
