How does this work?

Family members and friends of {{ $state.client.CouplesNames }} will make their best prediction about the birth of {{ $state.client.BabyAlias }}. There are five categories to make predictions about:

  • Gender
  • Date
  • Time
  • Weight
  • Length

Each prediction category is worth a certain amount of points. Once {{ $state.client.BabyAlias }} is born the two people with the most points will earn a reward.

What is PreggoPredict?

PreggoPredict.com is a website created by Josiah Peters to allow friends and family members a chance to make predictions on what they think Kim and Joey's first baby is going to be.
