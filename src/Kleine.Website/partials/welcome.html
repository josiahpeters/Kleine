How does this work?

Family members and friends of Kim and Joey will make their best prediction about the birth of Baby Peters. There are five categories to make predictions about:

  • Gender
  • Date
  • Time
  • Weight
  • Length

Each prediction category is worth a certain amount of points. Once Baby Peters is born the two people with the most points will earn a reward.

What is PreggoPredict?

PreggoPredict.com is a website created by Josiah Peters to allow friends and family members a chance to make predictions on what they think Kim and Joey's first baby is going to be.
